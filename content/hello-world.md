Replace this content with your own writing. You can write about anything — software engineering, personal projects, travel, cooking, or whatever interests you.

## How posts work

Each post is a markdown file in the `content/` directory. Add YAML frontmatter at the top with a title, date, and spoiler (short description), then write your content in markdown below.

You can also add a Chinese translation by creating a `.zh.md` version of the same file. A language toggle will automatically appear on posts that have translations.
